Spirituality

Spirituality is a sense of connection and belief in something beyond oneself. The subjective experience of a sacred dimension involves an idea in the supernatural realm beyond the ordinary observable world. It is where miracles occur, and "awe and wonder" is birthed. It is knowing that we are not alone, that we "call out for help" and know we will be heard. By incorporating ancient spiritual and mystical practices, you will learn how to receive spiritual guidance and support.

Theology

Theology is the study of God and religious belief and incorporates the systematic study of Christian tradition and thought expressed through worship, literature and language throughout history. By combining the Holy Scripture, including the Apocrypha and other missing books, along with the teachings of the Mystics, you can "remove the scales from your eyes".

Psychology

Psychology is the scientific study of the mind and behaviour. The goal of counselling is to make the unconscious conscious by incorporating a variety of therapies and mystical traditions to help understand human development over the lifespan. The role of nature versus nurture, emotions, memories, perception, family history, and desire influence our feelings, thoughts and behaviour. You will discover the real YOU through compassionate, explorative and meaningful conversation, dream analysis, creative therapies, inner healing, and synchronicity.

Philosophy

Philosophy studies general and fundamental questions such as knowledge, values, thought, existence, reason and purpose. We must acknowledge and understand why we interpret our current situations as we do. We look at the current worldview to differentiate between truth and myths so that we don’t set ourselves up for heartache and failure by subscribing to the "happiness trap". Instead, we need to value the storms in our life and embrace those learnings. As the Stoics say, "Obstacle is the way".

Soul's Purpose

Combining all the Gems helps you to remember the truth of who you are and then encourages and teaches you to embrace your purpose and share that with the world.

“If you are who you should be, you will set the world ablaze.” Catherine of Siena

Remember the truth of who we are, and then share that with the world.
